Ready to make and impact in the world?

Discover, Volunteer, Make a Difference.

Service Learning

By combining community service with reflection to teach civil responsibility and strengthen communities. Volunteer Inn promotes service learning in all of our projects.

Connections

Make friends from all of the world who share the same passion for volunteering as you! Volunteers can stay in volunteer housing or immerse themselves in the local community by staying with homestays.

Community Impact

Volunteers make real impact in the community with their participation in our projects. We have a full staff to help and guide our volunteers in their daily work and offer group programs and seasonal projects.

Culture

Understanding the culture of the community you volunteer in is very important to the impact you make. All of our volunteers have a project and cultural orientation on their first day of work and access to Spanish classes and tours.

About Us

Volunteer Inn Mexico with the Institute of Modern Spanish to offer volunteers from around the world inexpensive opportunities to immerse themselves in Mexican culture while supporting the needs to the local community.

Volunteer Projects

Childcare & Mentoring

Volunteers working with childcare and youth mentoring programs provide much needed love and attention to children and adolescents from different types of backgrounds...

Teaching English

Volunteers work in public schools and non-profit English language programs not only teach students, but to spark their interest in the language and inspire them to learn and continue their studies in the future...

Construction

Volunteers in this project work alongside locals in construction projects to build new structures, improve civil infrastructure initiatives and renovate existing buildings...

Environmental Conservation

In our environmental conservation placements volunteers work with prominent institutions and agencies concerned with the unique conservation issues of the local region.

Caregiving

Volunteers in caregiving projects work in shelters and clinics for the elderly and physically and mentally disabled. They make up for lack of staff to give individual support to the residents and patients of these facilities.

Animal Care

Volunteers work in local animal shelters alongside staff and other volunteers to bathe, brush, train, walk, and play with the cats and dogs to prepare them for adoption.
